Algebraic and Analytic Geometry Seminar (Fall 2024)

Welcome to the NYU Algebraic and Analytic Geometry Seminar webpage! The purpose of this seminar is to introduce students to Serre's GAGA, not necessarily talk about recent research in the field. The prequisites for this seminar are a solid understanding of complex analysis and algebra. We will introduce schemes and sheaf cohmology.
